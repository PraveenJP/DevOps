Why use Jenkins CLI?
------------------------
	-Easier
	-Faster
	-Memory management
	-Continuous integration

Step 1: Start Jenkins

Step 2: Goto manage jenkins > Configure Global Security > Enable security

Step 3: goto http://localhost:8080/cli

Step 4: download the Jenkin-cli.jar in that page.

Step 5: java -jar jenkins-cli.jar -s http://localhost:8080/ help --username VAL --password VAL
